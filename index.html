    Computer Science Club

    Welcome to the Computer Science Club! We are a group of students of a variety of majors (Computer Science, Game Development, Media Communications, Animation) Who are passionate about collaboration and getting feedback on coding projects.

    If you are looking to attend local tech events, showcase your coding skills, make new friends, or even learn a new programming language we would love for you to join us.

    Working on a project outside of class? Our club meetings can give you a friendly place to get constructive feedback on your passion project and actually finish it.

    Joining our club puts you in contact with upperclassmen in your major, Why wait to have a cool project assigned to you in class when you can get your feet wet by working with upperclassmen who can help you develop your project and your skills?
